> ***Industrial centrifuges use special high-speed rated Bearings specially made for centrifuges. Do NOT replace centrifuge bearings with aftermarket, 'off-the-shelf,' cheap bearings.***


---

These bearings are not suitable for the high operating speeds of industrial centrifuges. Generic bearings can lead to [centrifuge vibrations](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge-vibration/) and possibly catastrophic failures.
