# DMPX-014 Self-Cleaning, 3-Phase Disc Stack Centrifuge | Specs & Performance

## DMPX-014 Disc-Stack Centrifuge

The DMPX-014 is a specially modified Alfa Laval Disc Centrifuge designed to remove water and solid particles from light oil and fuel.

The DMPX-014 centrifuge has an operating speed of 7,600 RPM generating over 7,200 G-force. [Diesel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/diesel-centrifuge/) and [Lube Oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/lube-oil-centrifuge/) are perfect applications for this centrifuge.
