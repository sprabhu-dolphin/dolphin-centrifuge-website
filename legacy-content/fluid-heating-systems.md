# Industrial Centrifuge Fluid Heating Systems

## Process Fluid Heater

Centrifuges efficiency is directly affected by the viscosity of the process fluid. Heating before centrifugation is highly recommended for viscous oils.

Dolphin Centrifuge has designed a fluid heating system for such applications. Our Process Fluid Heater skids are designed for electricity or steam as a source of energy.
