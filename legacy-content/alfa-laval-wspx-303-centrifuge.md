# Alfa Laval WSPX 303 Self-Cleaning, Coolant Centrifuge

## Operation

The Alfa Laval WSPX-303 Disc Centrifuge is a 3-phase separator designed to separate [tramp oil and small particles from machine coolant](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/machine-coolant-centrifuge/).

The WSPX-303 centrifuge bowl rotates at 9,200 RPM exerting over 8,000 G-Force. It is a small centrifuge ideal for [cleaning wash liquids](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/industrial-washer-fluid-centrifuge/), [machining coolants](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/machine-coolant-centrifuge/), and de-oiling of water.
