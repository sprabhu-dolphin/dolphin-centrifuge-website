## Alfa Laval G2-40 Two-Phase Decanter Centrifuge

The [Alfa Laval G2-40](https://www.alfalaval.us/products/separation/centrifugal-separators/decanters/sg2/) [Decanter Centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/decanter-centrifuge/) is a horizontal de-sludging centrifuge built for the two-phase separation of **wastewater**, **crude oil tank bottoms**, municipal **sludge-thickening**, industrial sludge dewatering, **paint sludge handling**, etc.

With a 25 HP Motor, the G2-40 decanter has a **bowl RPM of 4,000** and can process **160 GPM of water with light sludge**.
