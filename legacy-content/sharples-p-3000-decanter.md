# Sharples P-3000 Decanter | Applications & Specifications

The Sharples P-3000 [decanter centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/decanter-centrifuge/) is a horizontal de-sludging centrifuge built for the two-phase separation of liquid from voluminous sludge. With a 14" bowl rotating at 4,000 RPM, the P-3000 exerts 3,150 G-force for efficient solid-liquid separation.

With a 15 HP Motor, the P 3000 decanter can process 25 GPM on wastewater sludge thickening.
