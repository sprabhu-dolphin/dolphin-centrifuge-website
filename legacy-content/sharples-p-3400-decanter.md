# Sharples P 3400 Decanter | Applications & Specifications

The Sharples P-3400 [Decanter Centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/decanter-centrifuge/) is a horizontal de-sludging centrifuge built for the two-phase separation of crude oil tank bottoms, refinery slop sludge, industrial sludge-thickening, municipal sludge dewatering, paint sludge handling, etc.

With a 25 HP Motor, the P 3400 decanter has a bowl RPM of 4,000 and can process 50 GPM on ethanol hemp biomass separation.
