## Disc Stack Centrifuges for **Low Sludge Wastewater**

Alfa Laval [disc stack centrifuges](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge/) are wastewater purification solutions for applications with low solids **(under 10%)**. These high-performance centrifuges remove particles **down to 1 micron** and separate **contaminating oils** from industrial and food industry wastewater.

The disc stack design enables continuous operation, handling large volumes with minimal downtime. Operating at up to **12,000 Gs**, these wastewater centrifuges ensure superior clarification and oil removal, enhancing water quality and environmental compliance. ## Decanter Centrifuges for **High Sludge Wastewater**

Decanter centrifuges separate solids from liquids in wastewater treatment, particularly in **high-sludge scenarios (> 10% solids)**. These devices handle large volumes continuously, processing high-solids content in municipal and industrial settings. Decanter centrifuges help reduce sludge volume and associated disposal costs in wastewater treatment processes.
