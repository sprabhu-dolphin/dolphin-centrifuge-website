The Alfa Laval MOPX 209 Centrifuge has a 15 HP Motor, a bowl RPM of 5,180, and can process 25 GPM of Waste-Oil. It is a self-cleaning, [disc-stack centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge/) designed for the continuous, three-phase separation of fuels, such as [diesel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/diesel-centrifuge/), [biodiesel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/biodiesel-centrifuge/), [waste-oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/waste-oil-centrifuge/), and [fuel-oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/fuel-oil-centrifuge/).

## Alfa Laval WHPX 409 Partial-Discharge Disc-Stack Centrifuge

The Alfa Laval WHPX 409 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning disc-stack centrifuge designed for the three-phase separation of diesel, [light crude oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/crude-oil-centrifuge/), and [light fuel oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/fuel-oil-centrifuge/) (LFO). With a 12.5 HP Motor, the WHPX 409 centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 7,125 and can process 40 GPM of #2 Diesel Fuel.
