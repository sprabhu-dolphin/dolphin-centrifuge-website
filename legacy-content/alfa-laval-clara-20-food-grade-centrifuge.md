### Superior Clarification Technology

The Clara 20 is specifically designed as a high-performance clarifier for removing solids from liquids:

✓
Clarifier Execution

Removes fine solid impurities from a single liquid phase with exceptional efficiency. Ideal for beverage clarification and oil polishing.

✓
Gentle Product Handling

Unique design ensures gentle treatment of sensitive products like fruit juices, wines, and teas.

✞ Note: All wetted parts are made of high-grade stainless steel (s.s. 1.4462 UNS S31803 / 1.4401 UNS 31600).

### Key Processing Applications

#### Edible Oil Polishing

Ideally suited for the final polishing of vegetable and animal oils, efficiently removing residual fines, gums, and soapstock to improve quality and extend shelf life.

#### Beverage Clarification

Provides high clarity for fruit juices, wine, beer, tea, and coffee extracts by efficiently removing haze-forming particles and suspended solids.

#### Fermentation Broths

Excellent for clarifying broths and harvesting biomass in industrial fermentation processes, maximizing product recovery and separation efficiency.
