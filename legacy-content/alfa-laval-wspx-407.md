# Alfa Laval WSPX 407 Self-Cleaning, Coolant Centrifuge

The Alfa Laval WSPX-407 Disc Centrifuge is a 3-phase separator designed to separate tramp oil and small particles from machine coolant.

## Operation

The centrifuge bowl rotates at 8,500 RPM exerting over 7,000 G-Force. It is a medium-capacity centrifuge ideal for cleaning of wash liquids, [water-based machining coolants](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/machine-coolant-centrifuge/), and [de-oiling of washer fluids](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/industrial-washer-fluid-centrifuge/).
