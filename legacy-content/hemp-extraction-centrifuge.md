# Hemp Extraction Centrifuge

**A hemp extraction centrifuge is a bowl centrifuge designed to extract the hemp biomass from the alcohol. It uses a centrifugal force of over 3,000 Gs to spin out the biomass solids from the ethanol. The separated biomass and ethanol are continuously discharged from separate discharge ports.**

The exponential growth of the hemp industry has seen a variety of ethanol extraction centrifuges for sale. Basket-type (washing machine-style) centrifuges are suitable for small-scale producers whose primary consideration is cost. Product losses are not a significant consideration at lower batch volumes.

## Advantages

Hemp Centrifuges offer multiple advantages over conventional **basket centrifuges**. Following is a list of some of these benefits.

* **Continuous Process:** Decanter centrifuges have a flow-through design that allows the hemp biomass to be separated continuously. The centrifuge continuously discharges the clear centrate (water) through a designated water outlet.
* High Centrifuge Efficiency: A decanter centrifuge has a high centrifugal force of over 3,000 Gs. An open bowl with a low RPM centrifuge cannot affect such centrifuge efficiency.
* **Wide Range of Sediment Capture:** The decanter-type centrifuge uses centrifugal force to separate a wide range of sediment from 50 µ to over 10 mm. The pore size of the insert limits a basket centrifuge.
* **Continuous Solids Ejection:** The decanter disc-stack centrifuge continuously ejects the separated hemp biomass without the need to stop the centrifuge.
* **Compact Size:** Decanter centrifuges are compact in size and can fit into small spaces. For example, a 40-GPM Hemp Centrifuge has a 3' x 7' x 45 (H) footprint*.*
* **Low Operating Cost:** The only operating cost of a hemp centrifuge is electricity for the drive motor. Given that the centrifuge is 'self-cleaning' and fully automatic, there is no recurring material (filter, media, etc.) or manual labor costs.
* **Durability:** Alfa Laval decanter centrifuges are highly durable machines with primary bowl parts made of 316 Stainless Steel. The centrifuge frame is also very robust, and it is not unusual for these centrifuges to provide decades of service life.

## Industrial Scale Hemp Centrifuge Process

[![Hemp Process Flow Diagram](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20600%20240'%3E%3C/svg%3E)](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/hemp-biomass-separation.jpg)

**Hemp Process Flow Diagram**

There are multiple ways to extract [CBD oil](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cannabidiol) from hemp. Typically it is a 4-step extraction process that is also used in the cannabinoid oil extraction process. The processing steps are as follows.

### Ethanol + Hemp Biomass Slurry Creation

The crushed (chopped) hemp plant is mixed with ethanol or other extraction fluid. Again, a stainless steel reactor tank with mechanical agitation is well suited for this purpose.

### Ethanol Extraction from Hemp Biomass

The hemp biomass (from step 1) is separated from the carrier fluid by a centrifuge or other separation methods. The separated ethanol (or carrier fluid) now has Cannabinoid Oil dissolved in it.

### Ethanol Polishing

![Alfa Laval Stainless Steel Disc Stack Centrifuge](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20450%20560'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

**Alfa Laval Stainless Steel Disc Stack Centrifuge**

This polishing process removes the remaining small biomass particles, waxes, lipids, etc. This is achieved by a high-g, self-cleaning type centrifuge (or filter) to produce clarified ethanol.

### CBD Oil Separation by Distillation

In the final phase, the carrier fluid is distilled or evaporated to produce pure Cannabinoid Oil.

If designed and used effectively, Centrifuges offer high efficiency for the **production of CBD Oil continuously to produce a high-quality, consistent product.**

### Warm Ethanol Extraction

Warm ethanol extraction of CBD oil from hemp uses alcohol that has not been super chilled. The ethanol extracts the CBD oil from the hemp biomass and is distilled to obtain CBD oil. This process reduces the energy consumption of the ethanol chillers lowering production costs.

![Hemp Ethanol Dewaxing Centrifuge C1D2](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20400%20524'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

**Hemp Ethanol Dewaxing Centrifuge C1 D2 (Food Grade)**

The inherent limitation of decanter centrifuges is the inability to process below the freezing point of water (32 F). This limitation is due to the possibility of environmental moisture condensing and freezing on the rotating centrifuge components.

Warm ethanol extraction removes this limitation and allows the use of decanters for the biomass separation process.
