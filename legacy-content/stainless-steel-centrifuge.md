**Stainless steel centrifuges are specially designed disc-stack centrifuges for separating corrosive chemicals and food products. A pass-through feature allows continuous processing through stainless-steel centrifuges. A high centrifugal force of over 7,000 g separates fine sediment down to 1 micron.**

The Alfa Laval stainless steel centrifuges are generally of the [food-grade (sanitary)](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/food-grade-centrifuge/) design configuration. This design means that all the fluid contact parts are made of high-grade stainless steel (316L).

![Alfa Laval CRPX-207 Stainless Steel Kelp Seaweed Centrifuge](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20505%20600'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

**Alfa Laval CRPX-207 Stainless Steel Kelp Seaweed Centrifuge**

Some centrifuges have a stainless steel sludge discharge chute as well. These machines are applicable where the separated solids are of value to the customer.

For example, the precipitate is a valuable extract that must have all stainless steel contact in food flavoring extraction.

These food-grade centrifuges are available in a three-phase configuration, making them suitable for fish oil, rendering,and other similar applications.

## Alfa Laval AFPX 213 Centrifuge

The Alfa Laval AFPX 213 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning, [disc-stack](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge/), stainless-steel centrifuge with all wetted parts made of 316L stainless steel.

With a 15 HP Motor, the AFPX 213 centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 4,150 and can process vegetable juice clarification at 50 GPM.
