# Alfa Laval WHPX 513 Self-Cleaning, Diesel Fuel Oil Centrifuge

### Overview

The Alfa Laval WHPX 513 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning [disc-stack centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge/) built for the three-phase separation of mixed fluids and sludge.

With a 15 HP Motor, the WHPX 513 centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 4,150 and can process 72 GPM of Diesel Fuel.

### Application

The Alfa Laval WHPX 513 Centrifuge is widely used in marine and industrial applications. Some of these applications are:

* **[Diesel Fuel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/diesel-fuel-purifier/)**
* [**Black Diesel**](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/black-diesel-centrifuge/)
* **[Intermediate and Heavy Fuel Oils (IFO, HFO)](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/fuel-oil-centrifuge/)**
* **[Waste Oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/waste-oil-centrifuge/)**
* **[Industrial Fluids & Machining Coolants](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/machine-coolant-centrifuge/)**
* **[Lubricating Oils](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/lube-oil-centrifuge/)**.

## Alfa Laval MOPX 213 TGT Total-Discharge Disc-Stack Centrifuge

The Alfa Laval MOPX 213 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning disc-stack centrifuge designed for the three-phase separation of [fuel oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/fuel-oil-centrifuge/), [hydraulic oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/hydraulic-oil-centrifuge/), [cutting oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/cutting-oil-centrifuge/), and other mineral oil and fuel.

With a 15 HP Motor, the MOPX 213 centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 4,150 and can process 40 GPM of Used Engine Oil at 180 F.
