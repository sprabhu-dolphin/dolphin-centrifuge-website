## Alfa Laval NX 418 B31-G Two-Phase Decanter Centrifuge

The Alfa Laval NX 418 [Decanter Centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/decanter-centrifuge/) is a horizontal de-sludging centrifuge built for the two-phase separation of **wastewater**, **crude oil tank bottoms**, municipal **sludge-thickening**, industrial sludge dewatering, **paint sludge handling**, etc.

With a 20 HP Motor, the NX 418 decanter has a **bowl RPM of 4,000** and can process **110 GPM of water with light sludge**.

### Applications

The Alfa Laval NX-418 Decanter is used for the following applications.

* [Hemp Solvent Extraction](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/hemp-extraction-centrifuge/)
* [Ethanol Extraction Centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/ethanol-extraction-centrifuges-technical-comparison/)
* [Waste Oil De-sludging](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/waste-oil-centrifuge/)
* [Crude Oil Tank Bottom Sludge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/crude-oil-centrifuge/)
* [Municipal Wastewater](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/wastewater-centrifuge/)
* [Pyrolysis Sludge Separation](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/centrifuge-pyrolysis-oil/)
* [Liquid Humus Fertilizer](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/liquid-humus-centrifuge/)
* [Sludge Thickening & Dewatering](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/wastewater-centrifuge/)

## Alfa Laval UVNX 418 B-11 Three Phase Decanter Centrifuge (Tricanter)

The Alfa Laval UVNX 418 Decanter is a [three-phase decanter](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/three-phase-decanter/) centrifuge specially built for the three-phase separation of fish oil, olive oil, animal fat-rendering, etc.

With a 25 HP Motor, the UVNX 418 decanter centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 4,000 and can process 70 GPM of Fish Oil and Meal Separation.
