## Alfa Laval NX 314 B31-G Two-Phase Decanter Centrifuge

![Alfa Laval Decanter Centrifuge System - Class 1 Div 2 for Herbal Extraction](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Alfa-Laval-Decanter-Centrifuge-System-Class-1-Div-2-for-Herbal-Extraction-600.jpg)

**Alfa Laval Decanter Centrifuge System - Class 1 Div 2 for Herbal Extraction**

The Alfa Laval NX 314 [Decanter Centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/decanter-centrifuge/) is a horizontal de-sludging centrifuge built for the two-phase separation of wastewater, crude oil tank bottoms, municipal sludge-thickening, industrial sludge dewatering, paint sludge handling, etc. With a 10 HP Motor, the NX 314 decanter has a bowl RPM of 4,000 and can process 25 GPM of ethanol hemp biomass slurry.
