# Alfa Laval WHPX 510 Self-Cleaning, Waste Oil Centrifuge

**Explosion Proof WHPX-510 Disc Stack Centrifuge for Crude Oil**

## Overview

The Alfa Laval WHPX 510 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning [disc-stack centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge/) built for the three-phase separation of fuels, such as [diesel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/diesel-centrifuge/), [biodiesel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/biodiesel-centrifuge/), [waste-oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/waste-oil-centrifuge/), and [fuel-oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/fuel-oil-centrifuge/).

With a 10 HP Motor, the WHPX 510 centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 5,180 and can process 42 GPM of Diesel Fuel.
