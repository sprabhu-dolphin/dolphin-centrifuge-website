# Alfa Laval WHPX 513 Clarifier Centrifuge Module with PLC Control Panel, Stainless Steel Pump & Pre-Filter

### Overview

The Alfa Laval WHPX 513 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning [disc-stack centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge/) built for the three-phase separation of mixed fluids and sludge. It is configured as a 2-phase clarifier for the separation of solids from liquids.

With a 15 HP Motor, the [Alfa Laval WHPX 513](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/alfa-laval-whpx-513/) centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 4,150 and is rated at over 90 GPM on light oils.

### Application

The [Alfa Laval](http://alfalaval.co.us) WHPX 513 clarifier centrifuge module **we currently have in stock** is ready for use in the clarification of TSS from [wastewater](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/wastewater-centrifuge/).

Other applications this centrifuge module is suitable for are:

* Clarification of [diesel fuel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/alfa-laval-diesel-centrifuge/) or [lube oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/lube-oil-centrifuge/). (Piping modification needed)
* Clarification of mining residual water.
* [Separation of WVO from water and sloids.](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/wvo-centrifuge-separator/) (Piping modification needed)

The centrifuge module shown below can separate particles down to 0.5-microns (metal particles) at up to 60 GPM flow rate.
