# Industrial Centrifuge Buyback | On Consignment For Sale

## Buyback

[Industrial Centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/industrial-centrifuge/) buyback by Dolphin Centrifuge is one way to monetize your used centrifuge inventory. Send us details & photographs of your centrifuge, and we will get back to you with an offer.

We can offer you credit for your old centrifuge towards purchasing a new system from Dolphin Centrifuge.

## Consignment

If a customer has a functional centrifuge system originally supplied by Dolphin Centrifuge or others, the customer may choose to send the centrifuge to Dolphin Centrifuge on consignment.

The customer retains ownership of the equipment on consignment centrifuges, and Dolphin Centrifuge will offer the centrifuge to potential buyers.
