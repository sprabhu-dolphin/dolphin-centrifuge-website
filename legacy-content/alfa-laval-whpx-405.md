# Alfa Laval WHPX 405 Self-Cleaning, Diesel Fuel Centrifuge

## Alfa Laval WHPX 405 Partial-Discharge Disc-Stack Centrifuge

The Alfa Laval WHPX 405 Disc Centrifuge is a partial ejection separator designed to remove water and solid particles from light oil and light fuel.

The WHPX 405 centrifuge has an operating speed of 7,600 RPM generating over 7,200 G-force. [Pyrolysis oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/centrifuge-pyrolysis-oil/) and [waste oil separation](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/waste-oil-centrifuge/) are perfect applications for this centrifuge.

## Alfa Laval MOPX 205 TGT Total-Discharge, Disc-Stack Centrifuge

The Alfa Laval MOPX-205 Disc-Stack Centrifuge is a self-cleaning purifier designed to separate water and sludge particles from mineral oil and fuel. The MOPX 205 centrifuge bowl rotates at 7,600 RPM generating over 7,500 G-force. This centrifuge is well suited for the cleaning of [diesel fuel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/diesel-fuel-purifier/), transformer oil, [quench oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/quench-oil-centrifuge/), [black diesel](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/black-diesel-centrifuge/), etc.
