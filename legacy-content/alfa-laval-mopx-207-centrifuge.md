## Operation

The [Alfa Laval](https://www.alfalaval.com/) MOPX 207 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning, [disc-stack centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge/) ideal for the continuous, three-phase separation of fuels and mineral oils.

With a 7.5 HP Motor, the MOPX 207 centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 6,325 and can process 32 GPM of Distillate.

The Alfa Laval MOPX-207 is a versatile, 'self-cleaning' centrifuge that is small enough to be manually serviced without the need for lifting devices (cranes, hoists, etc.)

However, it can process over 30 Gallons per Minute of condensate.

# Alfa Laval WHPX 407 Partial-Discharge Disc-Stack Centrifuge

The Alfa Laval WHPX 407 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning disc-stack centrifuge designed for the three-phase separation of fuel oils, such as [light fuel oil](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/fuel-oil-centrifuge/) (LFO).

With a 10 HP Motor, the WHPX 407 centrifuge has a bowl RPM of 7,125 and can process 25 GPM of ISO 180 cSt Light Fuel Oil.

Also, the WHPX-407 is a partial-discharge centrifuge. This means that this centrifuge is capable of discharging only the accumulated sludge and minimizing [fluid loss](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/centrifuge-product-loss-during-discharge/).

Similar to the Alfa Laval MOPX-207, the WHPX-407 finds many applications in all kinds of 3-phase separation duties. Some of the popular applications are:

# Alfa Laval WSPX 207 TGP Partial-Discharge [Disc-Stack Centrifuge](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/disc-stack-centrifuge/)

The Alfa Laval WSPX 207 Centrifuge is a self-cleaning disc-stack centrifuge designed for the three-phase concentration of water-based liquids such as machine coolants and washer liquids.

With a 7.5 HP Motor, the **WSPX 207** centrifuge has a **bowl RPM of 6,400** and can **process 20 GPM** of water-based **machining coolant**.

![Alfa Laval Industrial Centrifuge-Coolant](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20600%20360'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

**Alfa Laval Industrial Centrifuge-Coolant**

## Applications

The Alfa Laval WSPX-207 is very similar to the WHPX-407, except that this centrifuge is a concentrator. A concentrator centrifuge is designed to separate a small oil volume from a large volume of water or water-based fluid. Some of the popular applications are:

* [Machining Coolant](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/machining-coolant-recovery-pasteurizing-centrifuge/)
* [Parts Washer Fluid](https://dolphincentrifuge.com/industrial-washer-fluid-centrifuge/)
* Water-based Drawing Lubricant
